Research Overview

Research Project Scope

The research focuses on developing an advanced mock interview platform to enhance the employability of IT graduates. This platform leverages AI technologies, such as Large Language Models (LLMs), Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to offer personalized feedback, real-time assessments, and tailored recommendations. The platform aims to bridge gaps in traditional interview preparation methods by providing dynamic, personalized interview questions, confidence analysis, and communication skill evaluation​

Research Problem

IT graduates often struggle with the dynamic and rapidly changing IT job market, which requires a balance of technical expertise and soft skills. Traditional mock interview methods lack personalization and real-time feedback, making it difficult for candidates to identify weaknesses and improve effectively. This research addresses the limitations of existing interview preparation systems by incorporating AI to deliver a more tailored and data-driven approach

Research Objectives

The research aims to develop a personalized mock interview platform that utilizes AI to generate tailored interview questions based on candidates' resumes and job descriptions. It seeks to assess candidates' confidence through multimodal analysis of non-verbal cues, including facial expressions and body language. Additionally, the platform will provide real-time feedback on communication skills, focusing on clarity and delivery, while offering personalized learning recommendations to enhance employability.

Solution

Our AI-powered mock interview platform is designed to boost the employability of IT graduates by providing a highly personalised, data-driven interview preparation experience. By leveraging cutting-edge AI technologies, the platform generates tailored interview questions based on your resume and target job description, delivers real-time feedback on your verbal and non-verbal communication, and offers personalised learning recommendations. This advanced solution addresses the limitations of traditional mock interviews, helping you refine your skills, build confidence, and excel in the competitive job market.

Personalized Question Generation mimicing real world Interviews

The platform analyses resumes and job descriptions to assess skill similarities and clusters skills using machine learning techniques. Tailored interview questions are generated from up-to-date sources, ensuring relevance to the candidate’s expertise and industry standards.

Multimodal Analysis: Assessing Confidence with CNN Integration

The platform uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to analyse video recordings, assessing candidates' confidence based on facial expressions, eye movements, and body language, offering a holistic evaluation of their non-verbal cues.

Speech Empowerment Through Intelligent Verbal Analysis

Verbal communication skills are evaluated through speech-to-text transcription, grammar correction, and communication fluency analysis. The platform provides adaptive feedback to help candidates improve their delivery, tone, and clarity in real-time.

Personalised Skill Matching and Recommendation using RAG Model​

A Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) model extracts relevant skills from resumes and job descriptions, matches candidates with suitable job opportunities, and recommends skill development resources to close any identified gaps.

Tools and Technologies

Our AI mock interview platform utilizes cutting-edge tools and technologies to deliver an effective solution. We use programming languages like Python and JavaScript, alongside frameworks such as React and Node.js for a responsive interface. Machine learning algorithms enable skill clustering and tailored question generation, while natural language processing analyzes resumes. Speech recognition evaluates verbal communication, and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) assess non-verbal cues.
